Privacy Policy for Zoomly

Last Updated: January 2025
Simple Summary: Zoomly does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information whatsoever.

Zoomly is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting any data about you or your usage. We do not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Store your photos or camera data
  • Access your location
  • Send analytics or crash reports
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies

Camera and Flashlight Permissions

Zoomly requires camera and flashlight permissions to function:

  • Camera Permission: Used only to display live magnified views on your screen
  • Flashlight Permission: Used only to turn on your device's flashlight

All camera processing happens locally on your device. No images are saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

QR Code Scanning

When you scan QR codes with Zoomly:

  • QR code processing happens entirely on your device
  • No QR code data is collected or stored by us
  • We don't track what QR codes you scan

Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is:

  • Your magnification level preference (stored locally in app settings)
  • Basic app settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Zoomly does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data at all, Zoomly is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app store listing. However, our commitment to not collecting any personal data will remain unchanged.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data about you, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your privacy is protected by design.
